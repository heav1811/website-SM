PAR

Plataforma de apoio aos refugiados (PAR) is a organization of Portuguese civil society with a mission that is to give support to refugees inhumanitarian crisis. The organizations of civil society gathered on this platform take as its mission to promote a culture of host support for refugees, either in Portuguese society, both in countries of origin and transit.

The platform has two action projects: The first, a family project of reception and integration of refugee children and their families in Portugal. The second project "front line" which supports refugees in the countries of origin or neighbors, using labor institutions Support (Caritas, Jesuit Refugee Service, etc ..), raising funds to support local work with the population at risk as is the case of refugees arriving in Greece, including Athens and Lesbos.
